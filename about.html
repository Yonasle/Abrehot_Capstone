ABREHOT Library

The Abrehot Library, established by Prime Minister Abiy Ahmed, is a modern facility designed to provide a range of resources and services to the public. The library offers a wide range of books that cover various topics, from politics and history to literature and science, available for borrowing. Additionally, it has a unique feature, which is the reading corner. This cozy and inviting space is designed to encourage reading and reflection, offering comfortable chairs and cushions, and natural light, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Another noteworthy feature of the Abrehot Library is the enjoyable coffee shop. It is a social hub, offering a range of high-quality beverages and snacks, designed for people to relax and socialize. The coffee shop's aim is to create an enjoyable and comfortable space for people to build relationships, share experiences, and discuss ideas. The coffee shop makes the Abrehot Library not only a place for individual learning but also a community resource that fosters social cohesion and civic engagement. A comfortable reading corner is a cozy and inviting space designed for relaxation and enjoyment of reading. It typically includes a comfortable chair or sofa, a soft throw or blanket, a good reading light, and a small table or shelf to hold books and other reading materials. The atmosphere is often enhanced by the addition of plants, artwork, and other decorative elements that create a soothing and peaceful ambiance. The reading corner can be located in any part of the house that offers a quiet and secluded space, such as a bedroom, living room, or even a dedicated home library. Whether for leisurely reading, studying, or simply taking a break from the daily hustle and bustle, a comfortable reading corner can provide a perfect haven for relaxation and mental rejuvenation.
